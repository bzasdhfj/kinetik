## 3. Maker Comment Draft (The Pitch)

Copy, tweak, and post this as the very first comment once Kinetik goes live.

```markdown
Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm the creator of Kinetik. 

Like many of you, I've tried dozens of habit trackers. But they all felt the same: either they were cluttered with animations and social feeds, or they were slow web-apps wrapped in Electron that took up 500MB of RAM just to sit in the dock. 

So I built Kinetik. It is 100% native (SwiftUI/WidgetKit), launches instantly, uses under 20MB of RAM, and respects your privacy by storing all data locally on your device.

Key features:
- 🧩 **Interactive Desktop Widgets**: Check off habits with a single click right on your desktop.
- 📅 **Smart Schedules**: Set templates for everyday, weekdays, weekends, or custom dates.
- 🌙 **3 AM Rollover**: Sleep late? Your streak doesn't break at midnight; the day rolls over at 3 AM.
- 🔒 **100% Offline & Private**: Zero accounts, zero servers, zero analytics.

Kinetik is fully open-source on GitHub, and I'd love to hear your feedback! What habits are you tracking today? 

Let me know what features you want to see next! 🚀
```

## 4. Launch Day Checklist

- [ ] **Launch at 12:01 AM PST**: Product Hunt resets at midnight Pacific Time. Launching early gives you a full 24 hours of exposure.
- [ ] **Post on Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and GitHub**: Share the Product Hunt launch link with a short video demo.
- [ ] **Notify Launch Communities**: Post in build-in-public directories, Indie Hackers, and relevant subreddits (e.g., r/macapps, r/selfimprovement).
- [ ] **Respond to every single comment**: Keep the engagement rate high. Product Hunt's algorithm ranks active discussions higher.
